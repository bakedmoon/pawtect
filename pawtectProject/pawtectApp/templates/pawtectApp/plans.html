Pawtect coversmuch more than hospitalisation

Pawtect covers the costs incurred for fee, Emergency care, surgeries, laboratory tests, diagnosis care, Hospitalization/boardingcharges,prescribed drugs and medications. Inclusions under each of these covers are as under:

Major Illness Cover:

Cancer and Tumor, Cardiovascular System, Haematological System, Respiratory Systems, Gastro-intestinal disorders,Urinary system disorders,Lymphoidsystems,Specialized therapy

Accidental Injury Cover:

Unfortunate accidental bodily injury like fracture or broken bone due to hitting on wall, falling while running etc

Third Party Liability Cover:

In case of any bodily injury and/or property damage to any third party due to your Pet’s action during the Plan Period, we will cover you up to the plan limit mentioned in your Plan Summary

Major Soft Tissue Surgeries Cover:

Gastrictorsion,Intussusception, Splenic torsion, Megacolon, Pyloric stenosis, Malignant gastric / intestinal tumours, 4th/5th rib removal / Pericardial drainage, Diaphragmatic hernia, kidney / urinary bladder / urethra / ureter under general Anaesthesia, Aural haematoma, Surgical intervention on Eye.

Please note that Pre-existing conditions,vaccinations,deworming and routine check-up are not covered.
